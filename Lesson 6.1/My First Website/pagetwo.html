Sky Whales

By Brianna Thompson

Twice a day at twilight,

the sky whales come to play.

They ride amoung the starry tide

and race the silver line.

They'll dine on sardine stars

before its time to rest.

They rest on beds of clouds,

and dream of far of planets.

If you're lucky,

you might see them,

only to be gone

in the twinkling of an eye.
